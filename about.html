About

Digital-first real estate journeys.

Giraffe360 helps property professionals capture, create, and share complete listing content through one connected workflow.

One workflow to rule them all.

Property marketing should not depend on disconnected tools, manual production steps, and inconsistent workflows. We built Giraffe360 to help real estate professionals create the content every listing needs from one connected capture-to-content workflow.

Hardware, software, editing, and AI tools.

Our platform connects property capture, asset creation, editing, AI tools, and listing-ready outputs. From one property capture, Giraffe360 helps create photos, floor plans, virtual tours, videos, property websites, and marketing assets. All processed automatically.

Innovating property presentation.

We are building toward a more digital-first property journey, where real estate teams can move from capture to complete listing content faster & more consistently, with more automation, better tools, and richer ways to present every property.

Built in-house, designed to work together.

Giraffe360 controls the full capture-to-content workflow, from camera hardware and capture software to processing, editing tools, AI features, and final outputs. Because each part is built to work together, property professionals get a more connected, consistent, and reliable way to create listing media.
